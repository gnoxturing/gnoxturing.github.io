Our History

PT Panca Prima Maju Bersama (PPMB) - Uniform Specialist catering to customized and industry specific uniforms. Established in 2008, PPMB has grown to provide uniforms for various industries and institutions such as the Indonesian Government, Indonesian Police Force, Indonesian Military, BUMN (State-owned corporations) and other private organizations.

We believe that a uniform is a physical representation of the institutions' values and ethics. Taking much care and consideration into our products is essential to produce the best uniform for our clients. Supported by PT Apac Inti Corpora's modern machine facilities and technology, we can supply fabrics with a range of colors and guaranteed printing quality. We also provide uniform details and other form of wears such as custom buttons, badges, shoes, caps and many more. Our range of designers are trained to develop designs that not only reflects the client's needs but also provides a design that is inline with current modern and traditional designs.

Quality and commitment is our core values as a company and it is by these values we are able to thrive and grow as much as we can. PPMB is built on the backs of Indonesia's workforce and product, and we take pride in the fact that we could serve and support this country. Join PPMB's journey in elevating your company's standards, one uniform at a time.
